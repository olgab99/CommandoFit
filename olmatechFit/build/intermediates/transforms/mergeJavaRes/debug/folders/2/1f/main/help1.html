Welcome to "Commando Fit" - training program developed by personal trainer with more than 25 years of experience in fitness industry, and based on his personal experience, and experience of preparing his clients (military personnel, first responders and extreme sportsmen) to the physical demands of real life in extreme situations. This application is not a collection of exercise or workout programs, but a full year training program which was used to bring many of his clients to new level of fitness. We hope that this application will help you to stay fit and be ready for whatever life will throw your way.

The program organized in blocks of seven days (pretty much as weeks – but you can start the program on any day of the week). To achieve the best results - follow the program schedule, and most importantly, the order of workouts. Additionally, you can do individual workouts outside of the program.

The application contains calendar with proposed training schedule and saved workout logs. You can email logs and post updates on Facebook.

As you progress, you should adjust your fitness level on Interview or Settings screen for application to calculate weight and number of repetitions values recommended for you.

The application includes various calculators that you will find useful during your training course.

Let us explore application functionality.

MAIN APPLICATION SCREEN

On this screen you can choose between doing Complete Exercise Program or just a separate workout. We recommend you to follow Complete Program scheduler, but if, for any reason, you feel like doing just a separate workout, you can choose from following modules:

  • STRENGTH / ENDURANCE – these workouts geared toward increasing endurance of your body. This achieved by lowering weights so you can do 12-18 repetitions in the each set.
  • MASS / STRENGTH – these workouts geared toward building strength and size of muscles by increasing weights so you can do 6-12 repetitions in the each set.
  • TACTICAL – these workouts consist of mix of high-intensity cardio and power exercises that you have to do in order, without any breaks for the rest. These workouts provide excellent cardio, endurance and strength training all in one and prepare your body for function in extreme physical conditions.

After choosing the complete program or just a module, view available workouts on the Workouts List screen.

Recommended weight calculator

ONE REP MAX CALCULATOR

One repetition maximum is the maximum amount of weight person can lift in a single repetition for a given exercise. It is used to determine the desired "load" for an exercise (as a percentage of the one rep max).

Choose the weight with which you think you can do 2 – 15 repetitions, do set to failure, enter the weight and number of repetitions with wheels, then press CALCULATE button. Calculated one rep max value will be displayed in the text field and percentages in the table below.

This test should be performed with a spotter for reasons of safety.

TARGET HEART RATE CALCULATORS – GENERAL AND KARVONEN

These calculator used for maximizing effectiveness of cardio exercises by making sure that you are exercising hard enough to get a good workout, but not too hard to strain your heart.

Your target heart rate is a range expressed in percentage from your maximum heart rate (calculated based on your age when using the General Heart Rate Calculator, or on your age and resting heart rate in Karvonen calculator).

The ranges are split into five zones. Choose appropriate for you zone based on your fitness level. If you just recently started exercising, aim for the Maintenance zone (at 50% - 60%), if you are regular exerciser – Fitness / Fat burn zone (60% - 70%) would be right for you. If you are very active – Cardio training / Endurance zone (70% - 80%) will be good fit for you.

INTERVIEW SCREEN

This screen collects basic information about you to help the application customizing workouts specifically for you. This data is used only by the application and not forwarded anywhere.

You can input following information:

  • Select "MALE" or "FEMALE" radio button to display male or female images on workout screens. Some workouts have gender-specific exercises
  • Input your name, age and weight
  • Input Resting heart rate to provide more accurate calculations for cardio exercise zones. To accurately measure your resting heart rate: Count your pulse on your wrist or on the side of your neck prior to getting out of the bed in the morning. Count the number of beats, starting with zero, for one full minute. For accuracy, take your resting heart rate three mornings in a row and average the heart rates.
  • Select weight units "LBS" or "KG" option - to set units for tracking weights. You can modify it on Settings screen
  • Choose fitness level to use in application calculators. It is important to update your fitness level as you progresses for application calculators to work properly

SETTINGS SCREEN

On this screen you can set / modify following preferences:

  • Rest interval between sets - Following completion of each set, you can use timer to time the rest interval between sets – choose 30, 45, or 60 seconds interval
  • Play sound on time up – check it if you wish sound notification when rest and cardio time is up
  • Start timer on log save – check it if you wish to starts timer automatically when you tap Calculate/Save button
  • Advance to the next exercise when recommended number of sets completed – check it if you wish automatically advance to the next exercise after you completed recommended number of sets and saved them to workout log
  • Choose one of the Units radio buttons to use kilograms or pounds, miles or kilometres to measure weights and distances
